Our Story

Founded in 2020, Kodkiddoss emerged from a simple yet powerful vision: to transform children from passive technology consumers into creative tech innovators. We understand that today's digital natives need more than just basic computer skills.

Our journey began with a small group of passionate educators and tech experts who believed in making coding education both accessible and enjoyable for children. Today, we've grown into a trusted platform that parents rely on for their children's tech education.
